Why Orbi?

Let's keep communication simple

Orbi is built around a few core principles:

01

Your privacy matters

Orbi uses as little personal data as possible and avoids ad-driven product choices, tracking, and targeting.

An ad-free experience, with end-to-end encrypted messaging.

02

Events made easy

No digging through group chats for invitations - everything's right where you need it.

03

Friend-based connections

You decide who can reach you - only people you choose to connect with can message you.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Orbi?

Orbi is a chat and event app built for staying in touch without the clutter of traditional social media.

How is Orbi different from social media?

It is designed as practical media: no algorithmic feed, no endless scrolling, and no features designed to keep you hooked.

How does Orbi handle privacy?

Orbi uses as little personal data as possible, does not depend on ads, and does not use your personal data for advertising or targeting.
