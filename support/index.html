Privacy & data

Do you use my data for ads?

No. Orbi does not use your data for advertising or targeting.

What data do you collect?

We only collect the data needed to run the app, such as your account details and the content you choose to share.

Does Orbi use end-to-end encryption?

Yes. Messages on Orbi are secured with end-to-end encryption when this option is enabled during chat creation, meaning only participants in the conversation can read them.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest, and we design Orbi with privacy in mind.

Support

Why is there a subscription?

Instead of ads and tracking, Orbi is supported by its users. A small subscription helps keep the app simple, private, and focused.

Do I get extra features with a subscription?

No. The subscription is optional and supports the app. It does not unlock additional features.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your device's subscription settings.

Will I lose access if I don't subscribe?

No. Orbi remains fully usable without a subscription.

Something isn't working, what should I do?

Try restarting the app or checking your internet connection. If the issue continues, contact us at hello@orbichat.io.
